About Dangi Traders

Three generations of trust, quality, and service to the agricultural community

Our Story

Our shop was established in the year 2011 by Mr. Sandeep Dangi. The foundation of this business was laid with an understanding of the needs of the village. Today, the shop is jointly managed by Mr. Sandeep Dangi and his father, Mr. Manak Singh Dangi.

In our shop, you will find a complete range of grocery items, animal feed, fertilizers, and storage sacks for onions and potatoes, all at reasonable prices.

Our goal is to provide all villagers with quality products at fair prices. We ensure that every customer receives good products and excellent service without any compromise.

We always strive to maintain your trust and prioritize your convenience.
