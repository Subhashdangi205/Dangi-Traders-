Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 21 August 2025

At Dangi Traders, we strive to provide you with high-quality agricultural products including fertilizers, animal feed, grocery items, and storage bags. If for any reason you are not satisfied, this Return & Refund Policy explains your rights and options.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of purchase/delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
  • Damaged, used, or opened items may not be eligible for return.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Perishable goods (e.g., groceries, seeds once opened).
  • Customized or special-order products.
  • Items damaged due to mishandling after delivery.

3. Process for Returns

To request a return:

  1. Contact us via phone or WhatsApp within 7 days.
  2. Provide your invoice/order details and reason for return.
  3. Bring/ship the item to our store at your own expense (unless product was defective or incorrect).

4. Refunds

  • Refunds will be issued only after inspection and approval of returned goods.
  • Refund method: original payment method (cash/UPI/bank transfer).
  • Processing time: up to 7 business days.

5. Exchange Policy

In certain cases, you may choose to exchange the item for another product of equal or higher value (by paying the difference). Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
